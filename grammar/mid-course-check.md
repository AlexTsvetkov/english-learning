## 🎯 Grammar Diagnostic Quiz

### Section A: Choose the Correct Form

1. She _______ a therapist since January.
   a) sees  b) has seen  c) has been seeing  d) saw

2. He _______ that he felt responsible for the breakup.
   a) denied  b) suggested  c) admitted  d) encouraged

3. If she _______ boundaries earlier, she wouldn't be so exhausted now.
   a) set  b) had set  c) would set  d) has set

4. She looks upset. She _______ received bad news.
   a) must  b) must have  c) should  d) should have

5. I regret _______ therapy sooner.
   a) not to start  b) to not start  c) not starting  d) not start

6. It _______ that childhood trauma affects brain development.
   a) believes  b) is believed  c) has believed  d) was believing

7. He claimed that he _______ fine, but everyone could see he was struggling.
   a) is  b) was  c) has been  d) will be

8. If I _______ more assertive, I would have spoken up during the meeting.
   a) were  b) had been  c) would be  d) am

9. She suggested _______ a journal to track her emotions.
   a) to keep  b) keeping  c) to keeping  d) keep

10. New treatments for anxiety _______ by researchers right now.
    a) develop  b) are developed  c) are being developed  d) have developed

<details><summary>✅ Answers</summary>

1. **c)** has been seeing (ongoing since January)
2. **c)** admitted (confessing reluctantly)
3. **b)** had set (mixed conditional: past → present)
4. **b)** must have (past deduction)
5. **c)** not starting (regret + -ing)
6. **b)** is believed (impersonal passive)
7. **b)** was (reported speech tense shift)
8. **a)** were (mixed conditional: present → past)
9. **b)** keeping (suggest + -ing)
10. **c)** are being developed (present continuous passive)
</details>

### Section B: Error Correction

1. I have went through a very difficult period last year.
2. She suggested me to try mindfulness meditation.
3. If I would know about attachment styles, I would understand myself better.
4. He mustn't be happy in that relationship.
5. It is widely believed, that resilience can be developed.
6. She denied that she wasn't feeling anxious.

<details><summary>✅ Answers</summary>

1. I **went** through a very difficult period last year. (Past Simple — specific time)
2. She suggested **trying** mindfulness meditation. (suggest + -ing)
3. If I **knew** about attachment styles, I would understand myself better. (Second conditional)
4. He **can't** be happy in that relationship. (can't for negative deduction)
5. It is widely believed **that** resilience can be developed. (no comma before that)
6. She denied **that she was** feeling anxious. / She denied **feeling** anxious. (deny doesn't use double negative)
</details>
