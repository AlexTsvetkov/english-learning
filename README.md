# English Improvement Course: Psychology & Personal Growth

## 12-Week B1→B2+/C1 Course for Russian Speakers

A comprehensive English course combining **grammar**, **vocabulary**, and **essay writing** — all centered around psychology, mental health, emotions, and personal growth.

---

## Course Structure

Each week contains 3 modules:
- 📘 **Grammar** (`grammar/`) — ~45 min
- 📗 **Vocabulary** (`vocabulary/`) — ~30 min  
- 📙 **Writing** (`essay/`) — ~45 min

## Weekly Schedule (Recommended)

| Day | Activity | Time |
|-----|----------|------|
| Monday | Grammar Module | 45 min |
| Tuesday | Vocabulary Module | 30 min |
| Wednesday | Writing Module | 45 min |
| Thursday | Review + Self-Reflection Journal | 20 min |
| Friday | Free writing or re-read model texts | 15 min |
| Weekend | Review vocabulary cards, revisit grammar notes | 15 min |

| 9 | Self-Esteem & Identity | Inversion for emphasis | Cohesion & linking |
| 10 | Grief & Acceptance | Participle clauses | Persuasive writing |
| 11 | Purpose & Meaning | Advanced connectors & discourse markers | Introductions & conclusions |
| 12 | Your Growth Narrative | Review & consolidation | Self-editing & revision |

---

## How to Use This Course

1. **Go week by week** — don't skip ahead
2. **Keep a vocabulary notebook** — write new words with collocations and example sentences
3. **Actually write the essays** — this is where the biggest growth happens
4. **Read your writing aloud** — catches mistakes and builds fluency
5. **Use the self-reflection journal** — even 5 minutes of free writing helps
6. **Review weekly** — every Sunday, spend 15 min reviewing the past week
